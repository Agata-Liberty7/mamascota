# Official product identity

## Product

Mamascota

## One-line description

Mamascota is an AI-powered communication platform designed for conversations between people caring for pets and veterinary professionals.

## Mission

Helping people be clearly understood in veterinary care.

## Core value

Mamascota helps people:

- organize relevant information about what is happening with their pet;
- explain observations and chronology clearly;
- find the right words and veterinary context;
- communicate confidently with veterinary professionals;
- overcome language barriers;
- prepare a structured report for professional veterinary care.

Mamascota does not replace veterinary professionals or compete to provide diagnoses. Its role is to support clear, structured, veterinary-contextual communication.

# Official short description

Mamascota guides people caring for pets through a structured AI-powered conversation, helps them organize relevant information, and prepares a clear report for a veterinary professional.

It supports people who need help explaining what is happening, remembering important details, communicating across languages, or feeling more confident during a veterinary conversation.

People can communicate naturally with Mamascota in virtually any language supported by the underlying AI model. The application interface and veterinary reports are currently available in fourteen supported languages.

Mamascota does not diagnose diseases, prescribe treatment, or replace veterinary professionals.

# Official long description

Veterinary communication often begins before a veterinary professional sees the animal.

People may need to remember when changes started, what happened first, how the situation developed, which details may be relevant, and how to explain everything clearly. This can become even more difficult when the person caring for the pet and the veterinary professional communicate in different languages.

Mamascota was created to make this communication easier.

Through a structured AI-guided conversation, Mamascota helps people organize relevant information, describe what is happening clearly, and prepare a structured report for a veterinary professional.

The platform supports different communication needs. Some people need help finding the right terminology. Others need to explain events in a clear sequence, remember important details, communicate across languages, or feel more confident during a veterinary conversation.

Users can communicate naturally with Mamascota in virtually any language supported by the underlying AI model.

The application interface and veterinary reports are currently available in fourteen supported languages.

Mamascota does not diagnose diseases, prescribe treatment, or replace professional veterinary care.

Its role is to support clear, structured, veterinary-contextual communication.
