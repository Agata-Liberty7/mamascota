## Short description

## Full description

Veterinary communication often begins before a veterinary professional sees the animal.

People may need to remember when changes started, what happened first, how the situation developed, which details may be relevant, and how to explain everything clearly. This can become even more difficult when the person caring for the pet and the veterinary professional communicate in different languages.

Mamascota was created to make this communication easier.

Through a structured AI-guided conversation, Mamascota helps people organize relevant information, describe what is happening clearly, and prepare a structured report for a veterinary professional.

The platform supports different communication needs. Some people need help finding the right terminology. Others need to explain events in a clear sequence, communicate across languages, remember important details, or feel more confident during a veterinary conversation.

People can communicate naturally with Mamascota in virtually any language supported by the underlying AI model. The application interface and veterinary reports are currently available in fourteen supported languages.

Mamascota can support communication before consultations in a veterinary clinic, during a home visit, remotely, or in another professional consultation format.

Mamascota:

- supports structured communication about dogs and cats;
- guides the conversation step by step;
- helps organize observations, chronology, and relevant context;
- helps people explain what is happening clearly and confidently;
- supports communication across language barriers;
- creates a structured report for a veterinary professional;
- provides an interface and reports in fourteen languages;
- supports urgent-sign navigation;
- does not diagnose diseases;
- does not prescribe medication or treatment;
- does not replace professional veterinary care.

## FAQ

### What is Mamascota?

Mamascota is an AI-powered communication platform that helps people caring for pets organize relevant information and communicate it clearly and confidently to veterinary professionals, including across language barriers.

### Does Mamascota diagnose diseases?

No. Mamascota does not diagnose, prescribe medication, or replace professional veterinary care.

### What does Mamascota create?

Mamascota creates a structured report that can support communication with a veterinarian.

### Does Mamascota support multiple languages?

Yes. People can communicate naturally with Mamascota in virtually any language supported by the underlying AI model. The application interface and veterinary reports currently support fourteen languages.

### Can Mamascota help when the veterinarian speaks another language?

Yes. Helping people overcome language barriers with veterinary professionals is one of Mamascota's core purposes. Users can describe observations in their preferred language and prepare structured information in a supported report language.

### Is Mamascota an emergency veterinary service?

No. When urgent warning signs are present, Mamascota directs the person toward urgent professional veterinary care.
